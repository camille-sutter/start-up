Connecting through food

At Jouy's Food Market, we don’t just sell good food and drink, we celebrate it. Connecting people, whether it be with producers or through a delightful meal, is at the core of our food society here in Jouy. If all you come with is an appetite, you’ll be able to sate it with meals from all over the world: Mexican, Indian, Lebanese or Italian, be sure to check them all! Here, you will enjoy how accessible local and ethical food is, when only 15km away from Paris. Don't forget to ask our traders and cooks about their favorite recipes here at the market, and tag us on Instagram @jouyfoodmarket when you recreate them!

OUR STORY

Our story began in 2023, when a market took shape in the outskirts of Versailles. The area was missing a sens of community - a market where workers, producers and visitors alike could enjoy food at all times. Our mission is to prove that food can be good, beautiful and respective of the eenvironment and the ecosystems.
